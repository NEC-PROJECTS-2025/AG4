Key Metrics:

  • Accuracy: Measures the proportion of correctly classified instances to the total instances.
  • Precision: Proportion of true positives among all positive predictions made by the model.
  • Recall (Sensitivity): Proportion of actual positives correctly identified by the model.
  • F1-Score: Harmonic mean of precision and recall.
  • AUC-ROC: Represents the classifier's ability to distinguish between classes.
  • Average Distribution Accuracy: Average accuracy of the classifier over multiple iterations.
  • Mean Fitness Function: Average fitness value obtained after multiple iterations.
  • Worst Fitness Function: Worst (maximum error) fitness value observed during iterations.
  • Average Selected Features: Average number of features selected during optimization.
  • Best Fitness Activity: Optimal (best) fitness value across iterations.
  • Average Computation Time: Average time required to execute the algorithm.
